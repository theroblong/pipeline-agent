# Standard Sales Enablement Output Pack

Use this catalog when an agent needs to generate repeatable sales assets for a product, service, buyer persona, industry, or opportunity stage.

## Standard Pack Per Offer

| Output | Purpose | Best Stage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| One-page executive brief | Give leaders a clear summary of problem, value, and next step | discovery, solution-fit |
| Buyer-specific pitch | Adapt the message for CIO, CDO, CFO, COO, CEO, or business leader | lead-capture, discovery |
| Discovery guide | Prepare questions and qualification criteria | discovery |
| Qualification checklist | Decide whether to advance, nurture, or disqualify | discovery |
| Objection battlecard | Prepare credible responses and proof needs | discovery, solution-fit, proposal |
| Demo script | Show the most relevant workflow or story | solution-fit |
| Pilot proposal | Define a narrow path to value | solution-fit, proposal |
| SOW template | Convert scope into commercial structure | proposal |
| ROI hypothesis | Estimate value and cost of inaction | solution-fit, proposal |
| Follow-up email sequence | Keep momentum after meetings | all active stages |
| Executive one-pager | Transmit Pruvida's worldview and proof in a concise leave-behind | lead-capture, discovery |
| Outreach campaign angle | Turn market timing, buyer role, or business moment into targeted outreach | lead-capture |

## Prospect-Specific Packets

Use `knowledge/sales-enablement/prospect-packets/` for draft account-specific assets generated from transcripts, conversation notes, or opportunity pages. These packets should remain `draft-internal` until stakeholder names, company facts, claims, and recommended next steps are verified.

## Required Inputs

- Product or service page from `knowledge/products/`
- Product marketing library from `knowledge/sales-enablement/product-marketing-library.md`
- Executive one-pager pattern from `knowledge/sales-enablement/pruvida-executive-one-pager.md`, when relevant
- Campaign source material from `knowledge/campaigns/`, when relevant
- Target account or opportunity page, if available
- Buyer persona
- Industry or domain
- Current pipeline stage
- Transcript evaluation or conversation notes, if available
- Requested asset type

## Operating Rules

- Identify the product or offer being positioned.
- Identify the target buyer persona.
- Translate features into business outcomes.
- Include discovery questions when the asset supports qualification.
- Include likely objections and proof needed when the asset is buyer-facing.
- Include a recommended next step.
- Use executive-ready language when writing for CIO, CDO, CFO, COO, CEO, board, or sponsor audiences.
- Use the product page's messaging pillars, packaging ideas, and recommended metrics when they match the buyer and stage.
- Keep the tone consultative, credible, and outcome-oriented.
- Avoid overclaiming. Use language such as "can help", "is designed to", and "is intended to" unless the capability is already proven.
- For SOW or proposal content, separate assumptions, dependencies, deliverables, exclusions, and approval needs.

## Priority Assets To Build First

1. Account brief
2. Discovery guide
3. Executive follow-up
4. SOW or proposal draft
5. Objection battlecard
