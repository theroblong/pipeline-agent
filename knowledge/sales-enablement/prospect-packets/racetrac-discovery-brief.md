# RaceTrac Discovery Brief

## Internal Objective

Prepare for a discovery conversation about driver efficiency, food cost analytics, and onboarding automation. The goal is to confirm the highest-value starting point and identify whether a 90-day pilot is credible.

## Recommended Opening Angle

RaceTrac appears to have several operational cost levers that may be measurable but not yet explainable: driver dwell time, idle fuel cost, overtime, food cost, and onboarding delays. Pruvida can help determine where better data, predictive analytics, and workflow automation can produce measurable savings.

## Discovery Themes

### Logistics And Driver Efficiency

- How is driver stop time measured today?
- What is the current average stop duration by route, driver, store, and delivery type?
- Which root causes are visible today, and which are unknown?
- How much cost is tied to idle fuel, overtime, route delay, and repeated training?
- What would count as a meaningful reduction in stop time?

### Food Cost And Store Operations

- Which food cost categories create the most volatility?
- How are purchasing, storage, waste, and demand forecast today?
- Which factors are already modeled: date, store, weather, fuel price, traffic, promotion, region?
- What decisions would improve if food cost and demand predictions were more reliable?

### Workforce And Onboarding

- How long does onboarding take by role?
- Which steps create delay: background, education verification, I-9, staffing vendor handoff, manager approval?
- What staffing-company markup and turnover data is available?
- How much does delayed start readiness cost?
- What roles need a recruiting pipeline forecast?

## Offer Fit

- Aevah: operational intelligence and predictive analytics.
- Automation Consulting Services: onboarding, I-9, recruiting, and logistics workflow automation.
- Blueprint Studio: process mapping before pilot scope.

## Proposed Next Step

Ask for a 60-minute discovery session with operations, logistics, food cost, and HR/workforce stakeholders. Use the session to pick one pilot lane.
