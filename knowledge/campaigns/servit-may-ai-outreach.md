# ServIT May AI Outreach Angles

## Campaign Summary

This campaign playbook uses timely May business hooks to create LinkedIn and email outreach for AI discovery meetings.

The campaign is source material. Rework brand, proof, and claims before external use.

## Outreach Angles

| Angle | Timing | Persona | Core Hook |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Q1 Just Closed | May 11-16 | CFO, VP Finance, CEO | Q1 close surfaces margin leaks and cash-cycle gaps that teams cannot fix fast enough. |
| AI Tools Without AI Results | May 12-20 | CEO, COO, CIO | Many companies have AI tools, but few can defend measurable AI results. |
| Hidden Margin Recovery | May 19-20 | CFO, CEO, business owner | Recoverable margin is often hidden in slow receivables, manual processes, fragmented data, and approval chains. |
| Long-Weekend Operations Test | May 19-22 | COO, VP Operations, CEO | Holidays reveal whether operations run on people or systems. |
| AI Announcement-Cycle Strategy | Late May | CIO, IT Director, CTO | Leaders need a board-ready AI strategy that survives platform hype cycles. |
| Spreadsheet Season | Any May week | CFO, VP Finance, COO | Spreadsheet-heavy forecasting and reporting are capacity problems disguised as process problems. |
| H2 Budget Planning | May and evergreen | CEO, CFO, COO | AI budget cases need specific outcomes, timelines, and ROI language. |

## Persona Mapping

| Persona | Best Angles | Decision Concern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CFO / VP Finance | Q1 close, hidden margin, spreadsheets, H2 budget | Margin recovery, cash cycle, AR velocity, budget justification |
| CEO / Business Owner | AI results, hidden margin, H2 budget | Competitive position, board narrative, measurable ROI |
| COO / VP Operations | Long-weekend operations, spreadsheets, AI results | Throughput, capacity, automation, reduced manual burden |
| CIO / IT Director / CTO | AI announcement-cycle strategy, AI results, H2 budget | AI strategy, infrastructure decisions, board-level AI readiness |

## Messaging Rules

- Use timely business context to open the conversation.
- Keep LinkedIn messages short and non-pitchy.
- Follow with email 2 to 3 days later.
- Make the goal a discovery conversation, not a close.
- Replace ServIT-branded claims with approved Pruvida or partner language before sending.

## Proof And Claim Review

Claims in the source include margin recovery, faster cash cycles, and 90-day deployment. Treat all such metrics as unapproved until reviewed.
