# ServIT Business AI Performance One-Pager

## Campaign Summary

This one-pager positions Business AI as a business performance upgrade delivered on existing systems, not as a technology project.

Use as source material for Pruvida or partner-branded business AI campaigns after confirming brand relationship and approved claims.

## Core Message

Business AI is not a technology project. It is a performance upgrade delivered in days or weeks, built on existing systems.

## Outcomes Positioned

| Outcome | Value Message |
| --- | --- |
| AI-powered forecasting | Turn static reports into predictive, continuously improving forecasts. |
| AI assistants for existing systems | Add intelligent agents to ERP, CRM, and operations systems without replacement. |
| Real-time operational intelligence | Run the business on live data instead of lagging reports. |
| Revenue and margin optimization | Apply AI directly to growth, pricing, promotion, sales, and pipeline levers. |

## Delivery Motion

1. Pinpoint 2 to 3 high-value use cases tied to revenue, margin, cost, or speed.
2. Deliver measurable wins in 90 days through forecasting models, real-time analytics, and AI assistants.
3. Scale proven use cases across the business.

## Capability Stack

- Aevah Platform: enterprise intelligence OS for governed data, real-time analytics, and production AI agents.
- Blueprint Studio: agent workforce and execution design.
- AI Advisory: leadership alignment, use-case prioritization, and business case development.

## Claimed Metrics To Verify

- 10 to 30 percent forecast accuracy improvement
- 2 to 5 times productivity improvement in finance, operations, and sales
- Decision cycles reduced from days to minutes
- 90-day measurable win
