## Problem And Value

- Problem: Fiserv has customer data and workflow fragmentation across acquired-company portals and systems, creating slow customer experiences and internal process friction.
- Business impact: Slower customer submission/completion, poor UX, manual process drag, repeated cross-division work, and customer complaints.
- Desired outcome: Unified data and workflow layer that improves customer portal usability, reduces process latency, and supports expansion across independent budget groups.
- Value hypothesis: A focused paid pilot could prove Aevah's ability to unify a specific customer workflow or data domain in Fiserv's own environment, then expand by group or use case.
- Success criteria: Needs definition with Eric, Adam, and other stakeholders.
