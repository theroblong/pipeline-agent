# AI Transformation Services

## Product Summary

AI transformation services help organizations identify, prioritize, and implement AI strategies that improve operational capacity, reduce friction, and create measurable business value.

## Best Fit

- Target accounts: Organizations that know they need AI but need help deciding where to start, how to prioritize, and how to connect AI to measurable outcomes.
- Target buyers: CEO, COO, CIO, CFO, business unit leaders, transformation leaders, private equity operators, government and defense contractors.
- Target departments: Executive leadership, operations, IT, transformation, finance, business units, risk, analytics.
- Best-fit pipeline stages: Lead capture, discovery, solution fit, workshop scoping.
- Best-fit buying-cycle stages: `signal`, `problem-exploration`, `active-evaluation`, `solution-shaping`.
- Common entry points: AI strategy uncertainty, leadership mandate, fragmented experimentation, governance concerns, operational friction, use-case prioritization.

## Pain Signals

- Leaders want to adopt AI but are unsure which use cases matter.
- AI experiments are scattered and not connected to measurable outcomes.
- Employees spend time searching, reconciling, rewriting, or manually processing information.
- Capacity-constrained roles need better leverage.
- Governance, data access, risk, or change management concerns slow adoption.
- The organization needs a practical 90-day path rather than abstract AI strategy.

## Outcomes And Value

- Clear AI opportunity assessment.
- Prioritized roadmap with 90-day, 6-month, and 12-month actions.
- Practical AI solution designs tied to business workflows.
- Better governance, adoption, and risk planning.
- Measurable ROI hypotheses and success criteria.
- Executive alignment around AI investment.

## Positioning

### Customer-Facing Positioning

Pruvida can help your team assess business friction, prioritize AI opportunities, define adoption plans, and implement practical AI solutions tied to measurable outcomes.

### Discovery Positioning

If the prospect is asking broad AI questions before naming a specific workflow, AI transformation services may be the right entry point.

## Qualification Questions

- What business process has the most manual work or decision friction?
- Where do employees spend time searching, reconciling, or rewriting information?
- Which roles are capacity-constrained?
- What would you automate if you had a trusted AI assistant?
- Are you focused on cost reduction, revenue growth, speed, quality, or risk?
- What data would AI need access to?
- What systems or workflows would AI need to interact with?
- What governance concerns do you have?
- Who would sponsor an AI initiative?
- What would make the first 90 days successful?
