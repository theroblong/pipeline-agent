## Company

Pruvida helps organizations adopt practical AI, automation, and operating-system improvements that make work easier to understand, manage, and scale.

## Offerings

### Aevah

Aevah is positioned as a Pruvida product offering for AI-enabled business intelligence, workflow support, and operational insight. Use Aevah when a prospect needs a smarter interface over business data, knowledge, process state, or decision workflows.

Detailed product page: `knowledge/products/aevah.md`

### Blueprint Studio

Blueprint Studio is positioned as a Pruvida product offering for modeling, designing, and operationalizing business systems. Use Blueprint Studio when a prospect needs to map processes, define operating models, coordinate teams, or turn business requirements into implementation-ready blueprints.

Detailed product page: `knowledge/products/blueprint-studio.md`

### AI Transformation Services

AI transformation services help leadership teams identify high-value AI opportunities, set adoption strategy, manage change, and implement practical AI capabilities with governance and measurable outcomes.

Detailed product page: `knowledge/products/ai-transformation-services.md`

### Automation Consulting Services

Automation consulting services help teams remove repetitive work, connect systems, design workflows, and implement reliable automations across business operations.

Detailed product page: `knowledge/products/automation-consulting-services.md`

## Sales Positioning Principles

- Lead with business outcomes, not model novelty.
- Clarify the prospect's current operating pain before recommending a product.
- Prefer staged adoption when the path is uncertain: diagnose, prototype, pilot, productionize, expand.
- Tie every recommendation to measurable value, risk reduction, speed, quality, or capacity.
- Preserve trust by naming assumptions and avoiding claims that are not supported by the current deal context.
